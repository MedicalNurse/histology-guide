Chapter 19 - Male Reproductive System

The organs of the male reproductive system are mostly located outside the body. These external organs include the penis and testicles (testes and epididymis). Internal organs include the urethra, seminal vesicles, prostate gland, and vas deferens. Their functions include the production of sperm, delivery of sperm, and the production of male sex hormones.

Testes

Testes produce both sperm and androgens, primarily testosterone.
